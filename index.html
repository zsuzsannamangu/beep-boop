Beep Boop

Type in any number, click submit, and see all numbers from 0 to your number in order!
However, if your number contains a 1, it will be replaced by "Beep", if it contains a 2 it will come out as "Boop" and a number with 3 in it will read as I'm sorry Dave, I'm afraid I can't do that." Note: A 3 will override your 2 and 1, and a 2 will win over a 1!
